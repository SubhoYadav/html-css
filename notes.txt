States:
state pseudo class usually comes into play when a user performs an action (no-aaction also viz. an un-visited link)

Following are the pseudo classes related to states:
The order of declaation of classes in this category are:
link, visited, hover, active

:link-> Used to select un-visited links
:visited-> Used to select links that have been visited

:active-> 

The :active pseudo-class is used to style an element that has been “activated” either by a pointing device or by a tap on a touchscreen device. It can also be triggered by the keyboard, just like the :focus pseudo-class.
